How to Use:
- Click the live demo link to open the calculator
- Adjust the Monthly Income slider at the top (range: $0 to $20,000)
- Drag each category slider: Housing, Groceries, Utilities, Entertainment, Savings
- You can also type exact dollar amounts in the number input boxes
- The pie chart updates instantly to show how your expenses are distributed
- Financial Summary box shows Total Expenses, Net Savings, and Savings Rate
- Budget Utilization bar fills up as you spend more of your income
- Future Wealth Predictor projects your savings over time
- Adjust the Projection Period slider (1 to 30 years) to see long-term growth
- Financial Health Tips section gives personalized advice based on your spending
- All values update in real time with no page refresh needed
